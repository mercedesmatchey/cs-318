Cheesy BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Pulled Pork
  • 1/2 Cup BBQ Sauce
  • 1 1/2 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 12-Pack Bread Rolls
  • 1 Stick of Melted Butter
  • Any Seasoning You Would Like

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF.
  2. In large bowl, mix together Cooked Pulled Pork and BBQ sauce.
  3. In cake pan, put the bottom halves of the bread rolls down, covering the bottom.
  4. In small bowl, mix melted butter and seasonings of your choice together
  5. Pour half of your butter/seasoning mixture on top of the rolls in the pan, evenly.
  6. Put BBQ Pulled Pork on top of the buttered rolls.
  7. Add Mozzarella Cheese on top of the pork, evenly.
  8. Add the tops of the rolls onto the sliders.
  9. Add the remaining butter mixture to the tops of the rolls, evenly.
  10. After the oven is preheated, add the pan into the oven for 10-15 minutes.
  11. Take out and serve!
