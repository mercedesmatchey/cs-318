Crochet Stuffed Animals

Patterns

Materials

This is the pattern for the 21 inch turtle that I made in February of 2024 (Photos soon)
I used white for the legs, head, and belly/body and I used a sage green for the shell.

Helpful Abbreviations

  • R: Round (R1 = Round 1)
  • SC: Single Crochet
  • MR: Magic Ring
  • INC: Increase
  • DEC: Decrease
  • sts: Stitches
  • FLO: Front Loops Only
  • BLO: Back Loops Only
  • (#): Number of Stitches in Round
  • x6: repeat 6 times

Head

  1. R1: 6 SC in a MR (6)
  2. R2: 6 INC (12)
  3. R3: (1 SC + INC) x6 (18)
  4. R4: (2 SC + INC) x6 (24)
  5. R5: (3 SC + INC) x6 (30)
  6. R6: (4 SC + INC) x6 (36)
  7. R7: (5 SC + INC) x6 (42)
  8. R8-R13: 42 SC
  9. Eyes between R7 and R8, 14 sts apart
  10. R14: (5 SC + DEC) x6 (36)
  11. R15: (4 SC + DEC) x6 (30)
  12. R5: (3 SC + DEC) x6 (24)
  13. R5: (2 SC + DEC) x6 (18)
  14. Close with a slip stitch, leave a long yarn tail for sewing
  15. Add stuffing

Front Legs (Make 2)

  1. R1: 2 SC in a MR (7)
  2. R2: 7 INC (14)
  3. R3: (1 SC + INC) x7 (21)
  4. R4: (2 SC + INC) x7 (28)
  5. R5: (3 SC + INC) x7 (35)
  6. R6: (4 SC + INC) x7 (42)
  7. R7: (5 SC + INC) x7 (49)
  8. Fold in half and SC the sides together
  9. Cut the yarn and leave a long yarn tail for sewing
  10. Do the second leg
  11. Second Front Leg Only: bring the yarn tail through to the opposite side

Back Legs

  1. R1: 7 SC in a MR (7)
  2. R2: 7 INC (14)
  3. R3: (1 SC + INC) x7 (21)
  4. R4: (2 SC + INC) x7 (28)
  5. R5: (3 SC + INC) x7 (35)
  6. R6: (4 SC + INC) x7 (42)
  7. Fold in half and SC the sides together
  8. Cut the yarn and leave a long yarn tail for sewing
  9. Do the second leg
  10. Second Front Leg Only: bring the yarn tail through to the opposite side

Tail

  1. R1: 5 SC in a MR (5)
  2. R2: 5 INC (10)
  3. R3-R6: 10 SC
  4. Close with a slip stitch
  5. Cut the yarn and leave a long yarn tail for sewing
  6. Add stuffing

Shell (Remember to Change to Shell Color!!!)

  1. R1: 6 SC in a MR (6)
  2. R2: 6 INC (12)
  3. R3: (1 SC + INC) x6 (18)
  4. R4: (2 SC + INC) x6 (24)
  5. R5: (3 SC + INC) x6 (30)
  6. R6: (4 SC + INC) x6 (36)
  7. R7: (5 SC + INC) x6 (42)
  8. R8: (6 SC + INC) x6 (48)
  9. R9: (7 SC + INC) x6 (54)
  10. R10: (8 SC + INC) x6 (60)
  11. R11: (9 SC + INC) x6 (66)
  12. R12: 66 SC (66)
  13. Put a stitch marker in the Back Loop of the first stitch of R12
  14. R13: 66 SC in FLO (66)
  15. STOP
  16. Before pulling through final loop, change color for the body (first color you were using)
  17. R14: 66 SC in BLO of R12 (where the stitch marker is)
  18. R15: 7 SC, chain 5, skip 5 sts, go into the 6th stitch, 10 SC, chain 4, skip 4 sts, go into the 5th stitch, 5 SC, chain 3, skip 3 sts, go into the 4th stitch, 5 SC, chain 4, skip 4 sts, go into the 5th stitch, 10 SC, chain 5, skip 5, go into the 6th stitch, finish out with 8 SC (66)
  19. R16: (9 SC + DEC) x6 (60)
  20. R17: (8 SC + DEC) x6 (54)
  21. Sew on legs (go all the way across)
  22. Sew on tail (go around each stitch in a circle)
  23. R18: (7 SC + DEC) x6 (48)
  24. R19: (6 SC + DEC) x6 (42)
  25. R20: (5 SC + DEC) x6 (36)
  26. R21: (4 SC + DEC) x6 (30)
  27. R22: (3 SC + DEC) x6 (24)
  28. R23: (2 SC + DEC) x6 (18)
  29. Stuff the body
  30. R24: (1 SC + DEC) x6 (12)
  31. R25: 6 DEC (6)
  32. Cut yarn and leave long yarn tail for sewing
  33. Sew on head (pin in place), grab a stitch on the body then go up into a stitch on the head, go down into the next stitch on the head, then go to the next stitch on the body
